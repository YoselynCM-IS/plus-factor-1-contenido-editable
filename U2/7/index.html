animals, actions, professions, etc. Which? To ask about a person

Where? To ask about a place Where is Mark? He is in the park When? To ask about time When is the party? On Saturday

How? To ask about the way in which something happens or about someone ’s health .

Why? To ask about the reason why something happens.
