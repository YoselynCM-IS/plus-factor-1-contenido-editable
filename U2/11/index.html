A – AN

We use the indefinite article a / an before singular countable nouns:

We use a when the next word begins with a consonant sound

a boat a nurse a year

We use an when the next word begins with a vowel sound

an actor an elephant an orange

NOTE:

a horse an hour (because the h is not pronounced)

BUT

an umbrella a university (because the u sounds like a consonant) We don ’t use the indefinite article a , an
