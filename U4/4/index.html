My space, my place

61

Expected Learning: Draw things in a room by a description.

WRITING: Description of a bedroom

I. Write in your notebook a full description of your bedroom. Be clear when you

mention the position of all the objects in your bedroom. Use there is, there are

and the prepositions of place.

SPEAKING: Description of a bedroom to make a drawing

I. Describe your bedroom to your partner. Ask him

to draw your bedroom on his / her notebook as

you describe it. Remember to use there is , there

are and the prepositions of place.

VOCABULARY: Colors

Colors work as adjectives to describe a noun.

I. What ’s your favorite color?

Because is used to introduce reasons. Because I like it.

Because it ’s my favorite color. Because I think it ’s beautiful

yellow                          red                          blue                          green

orange                          purple                          black                          white

gray                          pink                          brown                          beige

II. Answer these questions.

1. What color is the sun? 2. What color is the sky?

3. What color is your blood? 4. What color is a rose?

5. What color is coffee?

III. Colors are also used to express feelings, for example: I feel blue = I feel sad

I ’m green with envy = I feel envious

You are yellow = you are a coward

I ’m really in the pink today = I feel wonderful
