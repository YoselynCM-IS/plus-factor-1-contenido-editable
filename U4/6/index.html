Countable Nouns

• Can be counted.

• Have singular and plural forms. • We can use a / an or numbers

before them:

A bag – ten bags

Uncountable Nouns

• Can not be counted.

• Have only singular form.

• We can not use a / an or numbers before them: sugar a sugar ten sugars
